My Favourite Anime

I love stories about heroes, fast machines, and epic battles.

Demon Slayer

Tanjiro's journey to fight demons is inspiring. The animation style is breathtaking.

Cars

Lightning McQueen shows that speed isn't everything. Friendship matters too.

Planes

Dusty Crophopper dreams big and proves that anyone can fly high.

Iron Man: Armored Adventures

This show has cool tech and awesome suits. Tony Stark as a teenager is interesting to watch.
